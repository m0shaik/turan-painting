Our Services

BASIC REFRESH

A quick refresh service that includes patching, sanding, and touch-up painting on scuffed areas. Paint must be supplied by the client.

STANDARD COAT

This package offers patching, sanding, and a single coat of paint applied to walls. The paint color should match the existing home color.

PREMIUM FINISH

This package includes patching, sanding, and two coats of paint for a more thorough finish. Customers can choose their preferred paint color.

ELITE UPGRADE

Our top-tier service features patching, sanding, two coats of paint, plus detailed trim and door touch-ups. Customers have the freedom to select any paint color.
